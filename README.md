# 🚀 LaunchLens

**LaunchLens** is an AI-powered platform that helps entrepreneurs validate startup ideas before they invest time and money building them.

Instead of guessing whether an idea will work, LaunchLens analyzes market demand, competition, feasibility, and potential risks to give founders a **clear, data-driven picture of their idea’s viability.**

---

## 🌟 Features

- **AI Idea Analysis**  
  Instantly analyze startup ideas using advanced AI models.

- **Market Validation**  
  Evaluate demand, target customers, and market opportunities.

- **Competitor Insights**  
  Identify existing competitors and saturation levels.

- **Feasibility Scoring**  
  Receive a structured score evaluating the likelihood of success.

- **Actionable Recommendations**  
  Get suggestions to refine and improve your startup idea.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. Enter your startup idea.
2. LaunchLens analyzes the idea using AI and market data.
3. The platform generates:
   - Market insights
   - Competitor landscape
   - Opportunity score
   - Strategic recommendations

This allows founders to **quickly validate or pivot ideas before building a product.**
